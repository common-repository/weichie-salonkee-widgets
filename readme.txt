Show your openinghours from your Salonkee application directly on your WordPress website with a simple shortcode:
[salonkee-openinghours]

== Installation ==
Install this plugin.

Activate the plugin.

Use the shortcode [salonkee-openinghours] where you would love to show your openinghours
